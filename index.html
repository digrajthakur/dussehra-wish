History Of Dussehra

The Hindu epic Ramayana, which tells the story of Lord Rama, the eighth incarnation of Lord Vishnu, killing the ten-headed monster
Ravana in the Sat-yuga after Ravana kidnapped Lord Rama's wife Sita, is the origin of the Dussehra festival With the assistance of his
brother Lakshman and his devotee Hanuman, Lord Rama travelled all the way to Lanka (Ravana's realm) in order to confront Ravana
and win Sita back. In order to receive the protection of the goddess of courage and strength, Rama prayed to Durga while they were on
the journey. Finally, Lord Rama defeated evil by killing Ravana. To commemorate this day, people mark Vijayadashami or the Dussehra
festival, and Dussehra celebrations are held all around the country.
